When will my order ship?

Here’s how it works. You order, we process, then we ship. While ordering on secondatebeauty.com only takes a few seconds, processing your purchase takes 1 to 2 business days for us (and orders placed after 16.00 WIB will be processed on the next business day). Then one of our carriers takes it from there. You’ll get a shipment notification email with a tracking number as soon as your package ships. Our love is on the way! If you placed an order on Friday afternoon, Saturday, Sunday or Public Holiday, it will be processed on the next working day after you finish the payment.
